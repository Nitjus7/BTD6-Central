About BTD6 Central

Much of BTD6 Central is powered by the Ninja Kiwi API, a resource provided by Ninja Kiwi to give developers easy access to in-game info. The OAK ID you provide for your player profile is never saved and can't be used to track any sensitive information. In other words, OAK IDs are safe to share and use.

Other info such as Paragon stats, Bloon stats, and Boss stats are provided by myself and some others. There may be some inaccuracies with these stats, so please report it to the BTD6 Central Discord Server if you have any concerns.
